## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Will I lose the money I paid to Wix?**
A: That money is already spent (sunk cost). You can't recover it whether you use Wix or not. Using Vercel doesn't cost you MORE money - it's free.

**Q: Is Vercel really free?**
A: Yes, genuinely free forever for websites like yours. No trial period, no credit card needed.

**Q: What if I want to switch back to Wix later?**
A: Just change the DNS records back. Your Wix account isn't deleted.

**Q: How do I update the website after it's deployed?**
A: Edit your code, push to GitHub, Vercel auto-deploys in 2-3 minutes.

**Q: What if something breaks?**
A: Vercel keeps version history. You can roll back to any previous version instantly.

**Q: Will my domain still work with email?**
A: Yes! Email (MX records) is separate from website hosting. Email keeps working.
